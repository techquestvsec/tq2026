Media & Arts

Frame D Flash

Create a 2D animation in Macromedia Flash 8 — topic revealed on event day, judged on timing, smoothness, and creativity.

Software: Macromedia Flash 8
Mode: Individual / Team
Topic: Revealed on event day
Overview

Participants create a 2D animated sequence in Macromedia Flash 8. The animation topic is announced on the day of the event. Storyboards are encouraged but not mandatory.

Judging Criteria
  • Smoothness — quality of motion and frame transitions.
  • Timing — how well the animation matches its intended pacing.
  • Creativity — originality of concept, characters, and narrative.
  • Original Art — use of self-drawn assets is preferred.
