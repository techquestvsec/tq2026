Design

PixBit

Transform stock images into digital art using any photo editing tool — topic and source images provided on the day.

Software: Any photo editor
Mode: Individual / Team
Images: Provided on event day
Topic: Announced on event day
Overview

Participants receive a set of stock images and a topic on the day of the event. They use any photo editing software to transform these into polished digital artwork. No external assets may be imported.

Rules
  • Only the provided images may be used as base material — no external imports.
  • Any edited work targeting an individual, caste, religion, gender, or community will be immediately disqualified.
  • Selection via pre-elimination test.
Judging Criteria
  • Creativity — imaginative use of the source material.
  • Technical Skill — mastery of editing tools and techniques.
  • Visual Impact — overall quality and impression of the final piece.
