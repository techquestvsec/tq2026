Scavenger Hunt

A hybrid digital-physical quest — solve layered clues, complete timed challenges, solo or as a team.

Mode: Team
Category: Junior(class 9-10) | Senior(11-12)
Tasks: Offline Tasks
Judged on: Completion & Speed
Overview

The Scavenger Hunt is a high energy adventure bursting with excitement, mystery, and nonstop action. From the very first clue,participants are pulled into a fast-paced chase where every discovery sparks a new rush of adrenaline. Hidden hints, unexpected twists, and cleverly placed surprises keep the momentum alive, turning every step into a thrilling moment. Teams race against time, piecing together clues, doubling back, and celebrating each breakthrough as they surge ahead. The atmosphere is electric—filled with laughter, urgency, and the thrill of the unknown—making every second feel alive. More than just a hunt, it’s an unforgettable experience packed with fun, teamwork, and the pure excitement of the chase.

Selection Criteria
  • Amptitude for logical thinking — number of challenges successfully finished.
  • Problem Solving — total time taken to complete all tasks.
