Design

Pixelated

Recreate a given image using only ASCII characters in Notepad — precision, creativity, and resemblance scored in 1.5 hours.

Software: Notepad
Duration: 1.5 Hours
Mode: Individual (teams of 2 if high registration)
Categories: Junior (5–8) & Senior (9–12)
Overview

Participants use only keyboard characters in Notepad to recreate a given reference image. The topic and reference drawing are given on the day. No internet access is provided.

Rules
  • Only Notepad may be used — no other software.
  • No internet connection will be provided.
  • Participants are not required to bring their own devices.
  • The final ASCII art must closely match the provided reference image.
  • If registrations are high, participants may be paired into teams of 2 at the event host's discretion.
Judging Criteria
  • Resemblance — how closely the ASCII art matches the reference.
  • Creativity — clever use of characters and shading techniques.
  • Completeness — amount of the reference image successfully recreated.
