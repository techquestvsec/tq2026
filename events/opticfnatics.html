Gaming

OpTic Fnatics

A 3-hour hybrid gaming event — 2 hrs of Minecraft challenges followed by a high-stakes TrackMania Nations Forever racing finale.

Games: Minecraft & TrackMania Nations Forever
Mode: Individual
Duration: 3 Hours
Starting Players: 22
Overview

22 players compete across two games in three hours. The first 2 hours are fully dedicated to Minecraft across four escalating parts — only the strongest survive to the finale. The final hour switches to a head-to-head racing showdown on TrackMania Nations Forever.

Players eliminated early in Minecraft are not eligible for the TrackMania segment. Scores from Parts A and B determine who advances.

First 2 Hours — Minecraft 🎮
Part A — 30 min
Time to Count Your EXP

Each player is summoned into their own singleplayer world. Survive, explore, and accumulate as much EXP as possible within 30 minutes.

  • No EXP farms allowed
  • You must explore the world — don't just grind EXP in one spot
  • Proper items must be crafted relative to your EXP level
  • Scored out of 30 based on EXP level reached
  • No eliminations this round
Part B — 30 min
Aesthetic Build

Build the most visually impressive structure you can in 30 minutes. Judged on creativity, design quality, and execution.

  • Scored out of 10 by the hosts
  • Judging takes place within 10 minutes after the round ends
  • Only players with strong combined scores from Part A + B advance
Part C — 25 min
Manhunt

Players who pass Parts A and B are thrown into a tense 25-minute Manhunt. Three mystery hunters — unknown to you — will be chasing everyone down.

  • You are provided a limited set of defensive materials and one attacking item
  • No teaming up with other candidates
  • You can kill hunters and eliminate other players
  • Eliminated players can respawn unless killed by a hunter
  • Try your best to stay alive — hunters are skilled!
Part D — 5 min buffer + fight
Arena PvP

Surviving players face off in a final Arena PvP battle. Last one standing wins the Minecraft segment.

  • 5-minute buffer period to equip whatever you want before the fight
  • No Crystal PvP allowed
  • Fight until one player remains
Final Hour — TrackMania Nations Forever 🏎️
Mode — 1 Hour
Take Over the Track

The top performers from Minecraft Parts A and B race against each other on TrackMania Nations Forever. Pure speed — no gimmicks.

  • Only players who passed Minecraft Parts A & B are eligible
  • Race against all eligible players simultaneously
  • Best lap times and race position determine final rankings
General Rules
  • Strictly formal language only — no informal or abusive speech
  • Be respectful to all players, hosts, and spectators
  • Teaming up with other players is not allowed
  • Tampering with another player's equipment is strictly prohibited
  • Spawn soft-locking is permitted
  • Defensive mechanisms for escaping hunters are allowed during Manhunt
  • Hosts' decisions are final in all scoring and rule disputes
  • NO ARGUING IS TOLERATED
