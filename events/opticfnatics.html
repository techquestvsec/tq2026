Gaming

OpTic Fnatics

A 3-hour hybrid gaming event — four escalating Minecraft challenges followed by a high-stakes TrackMania Nations Forever racing finale.

Games: Minecraft & TrackMania Nations Forever
Mode: Individual
Duration: 3 Hours
Starting Players: 22
Overview

22 players compete across two games in three hours. The first segment consists of four escalating Minecraft parts — only the strongest survive to the finale. The final hour switches to a head-to-head racing showdown on TrackMania Nations Forever.

Players eliminated early in Minecraft are not eligible for the TrackMania segment. Performance across all parts determines who advances at each stage.

Minecraft Segment 🎮
Part A — 60 min
Parkour Gauntlet

Each player must complete a custom parkour course within 45 minutes. Reach as far as possible or finish in the shortest time.

  • 2 checkpoints are available in the course
  • Respawn at the last checkpoint; if none, restart from the beginning
  • Players must stay within the course — no shortcuts or breaking blocks
  • No creative mode or cheats allowed
  • Completion time or farthest distance determines ranking
  • Top 14 players advance to Part B — 8 players are eliminated
Part B — 45 min
Aesthetic Build

Build the most visually impressive structure you can in 30 minutes. Judged on creativity, design quality, and execution.

  • Judged out of 10 by the hosts
  • Judging takes place within 10 minutes after the round ends
  • Only players who advanced from Part A can participate
  • Top 8 players advance to Part C — 6 players are eliminated
Part C — 30 min
Manhunt

Players who pass Parts A and B enter a 25-minute Manhunt. One hunter will chase all players while everyone fights to survive.

  • The hunter will be one of the judges and will be in creative mode
  • All runners can PvP each other
  • Limited defensive materials and one attacking item are provided
  • No teaming with other players
  • If a runner dies due to their own mistakes, they can respawn
  • If a runner is killed by the hunter or another player, they are eliminated
  • The main goal is to survive until the end of 25 minutes
  • Top 4 players advance to Part D (Arena PvP)
Part D — 30 min
Arena PvP

Top 4 players from Part C enter the final Arena PvP using an IPL-style playoff system.

  • 5-minute buffer before matches begin
  • All players receive equal kits
  • No external items or advantages allowed
  • No Crystal PvP
  • Qualifier 1: Rank 1 vs Rank 2 — Winner advances directly to the Final; Loser gets another chance
  • Eliminator: Rank 3 vs Rank 4 — Loser is eliminated; Winner advances to next round
  • Qualifier 2: Loser of Q1 vs Winner of Eliminator — Winner advances to the Final
  • Final: Winner of Qualifier 1 vs Winner of Qualifier 2 — Winner is crowned Champion
  • All fights are 1v1 and continue until one player is eliminated
Final Hour — TrackMania Nations Forever 🏎️
Mode — 1 Hour
Take Over the Track

Only players who qualified for Part C (Arena PvP) are eligible. All qualified players race together simultaneously. Pure racing skill — no external advantages.

  • Only players who qualified for Part C are eligible
  • All qualified players race simultaneously
  • Pure racing skill — no external advantages
  • Best lap times and race position decide final rankings
  • Player with the top performance wins the event
General Rules
  • Strictly formal language only — no informal or abusive speech
  • Be respectful to all players, hosts, and spectators
  • Teaming up with other players is not allowed
  • Tampering with another player's equipment is strictly prohibited
  • Spawn soft-locking is permitted
  • Defensive mechanisms for escaping hunters are allowed during Manhunt
  • Hosts' decisions are final in all scoring and rule disputes
  • NO ARGUING IS TOLERATED
