Design

Slide Master

Design a PowerPoint presentation on a given topic in under few hours — layout, clarity, relevance, and delivery are all scored.

Software: PowerPoint
Mode: Individual
Classes: 5th & 8th (Junior event)
Slides: 10–12 per presentation
Overview

Participants create a PowerPoint presentation on a provided topic within a time limit. A folder with optional text and images is available on each system. Use, modify, or ignore the provided content — your call.

Round Structure
Finale
Final Showdown
  • Top 12 selected students by the heads after selection will compete.
  • Topic will be given 15 minutes before the final.
  • Folder with topic related images will be available on the PC.
  • Each participant presents their slides to judges (2–3 minutes).
Rules
  • No internet access during any round.
  • No external storage devices or pre-prepared files allowed.
  • External help results in immediate disqualification.
  • Provided text and images are optional — you may replace or edit them.
Scoring
Creativity & Visual Appeal10 pts
Relevance to Topic10 pts
Slide Formatting & Layout10 pts
Presentation Clarity10 pts
Overall Impact10 pts
Total50 pts
