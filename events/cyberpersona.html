Cyber Persona

Design your digital disguise — a 3-hour mask-making event where art meets the digital world.

Type: Craft / Design
Mode: Individual or Team (max 2)
Duration: 3 Hours
Max Participants: 15
Open To: All (Sub-Junior to Senior)
Venue: Activity Room
Overview

Cyber Persona is a mask-making event where participants design a creative mask inspired by the digital world. Blending art with technology, students express ideas such as AI, virtual identity, and cyber culture through unique and imaginative designs.

The event runs as a single 3-hour round. Both individuals and teams of up to 2 may participate. Categories span all school grades from sub-junior (7–8) through senior (11–12).

Event Structure
Round Finale — 3 Hours
Final Showdown

Participants craft their masks entirely within the allotted time. The design must reflect a theme rooted in the digital world — AI, cybersecurity, virtual identity, online culture, or any related concept.

  • Planning your design and theme in advance is allowed
  • No pure painted masks — design elements must go beyond paint alone
  • No fully ready-made or store-bought masks permitted
  • Scored out of 50 marks by the event hosts / judges
  • Tiebreaker: the participant with the stronger concept explanation of their mask ranks higher
