// overview

Robot Snooze is a high-energy, team-based physical challenge where one student acts as the Guide and the other is the Blindfolded Player — navigating physical games using only verbal instructions from their partner.

Teams must rely entirely on clear communication and split-second coordination. Physical contact, gestures, coded signals — all banned. Only your words can steer your teammate through running, dodging, and unpredictable chaos.

Robot Snooze — The Gauntlet

One team member is blindfolded — the Blindfolded Player. The other is the Guide, who must direct their partner through a series of physical fun games using only verbal instructions. Teams are scored on coordination, precision, and speed.

  • Only verbal instructions are allowed from the Guide
  • The Blindfolded Player must keep the blindfold on at all times during active rounds
  • No physical contact, gestures, or non-verbal signals permitted
  • Teams must stay within the defined activity area on the school ground
  • Safe and controlled movement is required at all times
  • Roles may be swapped between games at the team's discretion

// rules & guidelines
ALLOWED
  • Verbal communication between Guide and Blindfolded Player
  • Clear and strategic instructions within limits
  • Pre-round team coordination and planning
  • Safe movement within the defined area
  • Role swaps between games
NOT ALLOWED
  • Blindfolded Player removing blindfold during active round
  • Physical guidance — touching, holding, or pushing
  • Gestures or non-verbal signals of any kind
  • Crossing the boundary of the activity area
  • Deliberate interference with other teams
