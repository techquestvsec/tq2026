Robot Snooze

One commands. One obeys. Chaos begins. — A blindfolded teamwork challenge where the only tool is your voice.

Type: Physical Activity
Mode: Team
Duration: 5 Hours
Max Teams: 15
Open To: Classes 5–8
Venue: School Ground
Overview

Robot Snooze is a fun, team-based physical challenge where one player acts as the Controller and the other plays the Robot — blindfolded and following only verbal instructions to complete tasks on the school ground.

Teams must rely entirely on clear communication and coordination. Physical contact, gestures, and non-verbal cues are all banned. Only your words can guide your robot.

Event Structure

Robot Snooze — Final Challenge

One team member is blindfolded — the Robot. The other is the Controller, who must guide the Robot through a series of tasks using only verbal instructions. Teams are scored on how well they communicate, how precisely the Robot executes, and how efficiently they complete the challenge.

  • Only verbal instructions are allowed from the Controller
  • The Robot must remain blindfolded at all times during the round
  • No physical contact, gestures, or non-verbal signals permitted
  • Teams must stay within the defined activity area on the school ground
  • Safe and controlled movement is required at all times
Judging & Scoring
# Criterion Marks
1Coordination & Teamwork20
2Control & Precision10
3Time Efficiency10
4Communication Clarity10
Total50

Tiebreaker: The team that completes the task in the least time ranks higher. If still tied, priority goes to better communication and coordination between participants.

Rules & Guidelines
Allowed
  • Verbal communication between the Controller and the Robot
  • Clear and strategic instructions within the given limits
  • Team coordination and planning before the round starts
  • Safe and controlled movement within the defined area
Not Allowed
  • The Robot removing the blindfold during the round
  • Physical guidance — touching, holding, or pushing the Robot
  • Use of gestures or signals other than verbal instructions
