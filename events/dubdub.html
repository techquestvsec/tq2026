Do DubDub

"Make the Room Echo"

Team Size: 2–4 members
Rounds: 1
Mode: Team
Open to: 5th - 9th
Event Type: General
Team Size: 4-5 members per team
Max Teams: 3 teams (post selection)
Format: Live dubbing in auditorium
Venue: Dance room + Auditorium
Selection Round

WHEN: 2 weeks before the main event

  • Participants are evaluated for vocal command and clarity.
  • Each participant is shown a silent video and asked to dub it live.
  • They are scored accordingly; the top 3 teams advance to the main event.
Event Day
Session 1:
Script Writing & Practice(Dance Room: 3 hours
  • The 3 selected teams assemble in the dance room.
  • one team head is provided a laptop containing both silent videos.
  • Teams watch the videos, write their dubbing scripts and rehearse for the full 3 hours.
Session 2: — Finals
Live Dubbing Performance (Auditorium: 30-40 minutes)
  • Teams are seated behind wings in the auditorium.
  • The first silent video(1-1.5 minutes) is played once per team;each video voices it live.
  • The second silent video (1-1.5 minutes) is then played once per team in the same manner.
