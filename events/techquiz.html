Tech Quiz

Two-round tech trivia — written MCQ exam followed by a live buzzer showdown with rapid fire, picture puzzles, and Tech Taboo.

Official Name: Tech Twister: Mind Over Machine
Team Size: 2 per team
Rounds: 2
Written Round: 90 minutes
Round 1 — Written Tech Exam (90 min)
  • Multiple choice questions (MCQs) + True/False + Fill-in-the-blanks.
  • Total marks: 100. Each MCQ = 2 marks.
  • Correct answer: +2  |  Wrong answer: −1.
  • Top 5 scoring teams qualify for Round 2.
Round 2 — Interactive Showdown
Segment 1
Buzzer Round
Each team gets a buzzer. First to buzz answers the question. Correct: +10  |  Wrong: −5.
Segment 2
Rapid Fire (One Minute)
Each team gets 60 seconds to answer as many questions as possible. No negative marking.
Segment 3
Picture Puzzle
Teams interpret a set of images to guess the single connecting concept (e.g. 4 images: lion, tiger, elephant, antelope → answer: Mammal).
Segment 4
Tech Taboo
One team member describes a tech term without using forbidden "taboo" words. Their teammate must guess correctly within the time limit.
