Weave The Web

Where Logic Meets the Layout

Mode: Duo
Categories: Sub-junior(class 5-8)
Teams: 6 (maximum)
Max Participants: 12
Format: Design
Duration: 1.5 hours
Overview

It is a rapid wire-framing challenge where logic meets layout. Participants will step away from the screen to draft intuitive website blueprints on paper, focusing on structural reasoning and user flow to architect the digital future.

Event Day

Participants will design a website using sheets, pens and colours.They will be given maximum 5 sheets. The website is to be made on one page which will feature a complete HOME PAGE layout, while the remaining pages will be used to detail the INTERNAL WORKING and logic of every function or button drawn.

Judging Criteria
  • Design Quality — visual appeal, layout.
  • Creativity — originality in presentation and structure.
