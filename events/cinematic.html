Media & Arts

Cinematic Cuts

Write, direct, act, and edit a short film — 6–7 topic choices given a week ahead.

Mode: Team / Individual
Topic: Given 1 week prior
Judged on: Creativity & Execution
Overview

Participants produce a short film based on one of 6–7 topics provided a week before the event. The film is judged on creativity, direction, acting, and editing quality.

Filming Rules
  • Shoot only indoors (your home) or in a public park — with proper permission.
  • Shooting near roads, traffic, metro stations, or crowded areas — immediate disqualification.
  • School shooting is allowed only with a teacher's explicit prior permission.
  • The Computer Department does not issue special shooting permissions.
  • Permission to bring cameras and props must be obtained from your class teacher beforehand.
Safety & Conduct
  • No props or items that can harm any individual.
  • No real-life violence or injuries to actors — immediate disqualification and further action.
  • All actors must be fully willing and aware of their scenes.
  • Background actors are allowed only in safe, uncrowded environments with teacher approval.
  • Any littering at the shoot location is the team's sole responsibility.
