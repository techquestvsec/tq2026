About

What is TechQuest?

Overview
A Festival of Innovation
TechQuest 2026 is the school's annual convergence of creativity and technology — spanning coding, robotics, animation, digital arts, gaming, and public speaking.
  • Open to Classes 5–12
  • Team & solo participation
  • Competitions + workshops
  • Judged by the TechQuest Committee
Rules
Important Notes
  • Ensure your events don't clash
  • Results are final and unchallengeable
  • Individual participation allowed unless stated otherwise
  • Selection is first-come, first-served
  • No preliminary rounds unless specified
Participation
How to Register
  • Go to the register tab and enter your scholar number.
  • Contact the event head if you face any issues
  • If you are unable to contact anyone then you can tell your problem on the email given below.
